OUR TRAINERS

Our trainers are highly experienced with more than 5 years in the physical health and training industry. They are proficient in the area of training and nutrition to help you get the results you want.

Johny
Johny is an experienced personal trainer with a knack for motivating clients to reach their fitness goals. His dynamic workouts blend strength training and cardio to maximize results in minimal time. With a background in sports science, Johny focuses on functional movements and proper technique to prevent injuries and improve overall performance.
Gina
Gina is a passionate fitness coach specializing in high-intensity interval training (HIIT) and functional fitness. With over a decade of experience, she brings energy and enthusiasm to every session, helping clients achieve their fitness goals while making sustainable lifestyle changes. Gina's holistic approach incorporates nutrition and mindfulness for a well-rounded fitness experience.
Sophia
Sophia is a certified personal trainer and yoga instructor known for her compassionate approach and personalized training programs. With a background in sports science and rehabilitation, Sophia focuses on functional movement patterns and injury prevention. Her diverse skill set allows her to tailor workouts to meet the needs of clients at any fitness level, promoting both physical strength and mental well-being.
